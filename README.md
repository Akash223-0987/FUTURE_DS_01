**Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship (Task 1)**

This project analyzes business sales performance using the **Superstore dataset**.

The project uses **Python for data cleaning and analysis** and **Power BI for interactive visualization and dashboard creation**.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze business sales data and answer key business questions such as:

- Which **products generate the highest revenue**?
- How do **sales trends change over time**?
- Which **regions perform the best**?
- What **categories contribute most to profit**?
- How can businesses **improve performance using data insights**?

# 📊 Power BI Dashboard

- **Total Profit**
- **Total Orders**

### Visualizations
- Sales trend over time
- Sales by region
- Sales by category
- Top selling products

The dashboard consists of **three pages**:

### Page 1 – Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard
Displays overall sales performance, KPIs, trends, and category distribution.

### Page 2 – Key Business Insights
Highlights key findings derived from the sales data including regional performance, top products, and category contributions.

### Page 3 – Business Recommendations
Provides strategic recommendations based on the insights generated from the analysis to help improve business performance.

---

# 📌 Key Insights

- The **West and East regions generate the highest sales**, indicating strong demand in these markets.
- A **small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue**, following the **Pareto principle (80/20 rule)**.
- Some product categories generate high sales but lower profit margins, indicating opportunities for **pricing and cost optimization**.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:

**1️⃣ Focus on High-Performing Regions**  
The **West and East regions generate the highest sales**. Expanding marketing campaigns and strengthening distribution in these regions can further increase revenue.

**2️⃣ Promote Top-Selling Products**  
A small number of products drive a large share of total sales. Businesses should prioritize these products in **marketing campaigns, inventory management, and promotions**.

**3️⃣ Improve Sales in Low-Performing Regions**  
The **South region shows comparatively lower sales**. Targeted marketing strategies and promotional campaigns could help increase demand in this region.
